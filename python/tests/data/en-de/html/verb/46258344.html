German

Alternative forms

  • verschliessen (Switzerland, Liechtenstein)

Etymology

ver- +‎ schließen

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fɛɐ̯ˈʃliːsn̩], [fɛɐ̯ˈʃliːsən]
  • audio175px(file)

Verb

verschließen (class 2 strong, third-person singular simple present verschließt, past tense verschloss, past participle verschlossen, past subjunctive verschlösse, auxiliary haben)

  1. (transitive) to close; to shut; to lock
  2. (transitive) to lock (something) up
