German

Etymology

zu +‎ kommen

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtsuːˌkɔmən]
  • Hyphenation: zu‧kom‧men

Verb

zukommen (class 4 strong, third-person singular simple present kommt zu, past tense kam zu, past participle zugekommen, past subjunctive käme zu, auxiliary sein)

  1. (with auf) to come towards, come up to; to approach
  2. to become, befit
