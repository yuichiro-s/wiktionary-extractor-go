Korean

Etymology

흥분 (興奮, heungbun, “excitement”) + 하다 (hada)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key)[xɯŋbunɦa̠da̠]
  • Phonetic Hangul[흥분하다]
Revised Romanization? heungbunhada
Revised Romanization (translit.)? heungbun-hada
McCune–Reischauer? hŭngbunhada
Yale Romanization? hungpun.hata

Adjective

흥분하다 • (heungbunhada) (infinitive 흥분해 or 흥분하여, sequential 흥분하니)

  1. excited
