Korean

Etymology

First recorded in 1459, in the Worin seokbo, as 걸이니. From the regular passivization of 걸다.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key)[kʌ̹ɭʎida̠]
  • Phonetic Hangul[걸리다]
Revised Romanization? geollida
Revised Romanization (translit.)? geollida
McCune–Reischauer? kŏllida
Yale Romanization? kellita

Verb

걸리다 • (geollida) (infinitive 걸려 or 걸리어, sequential 걸리니)

  1. to hang; to be hung
  2. to catch; to be caught
    감기에 걸리다
    gamgie geollida
    be sick/ill with a cold
  3. to become involved in something
  4. to take (time)
    차로 한 시간쯤 걸려요.
    Charo han siganjjeum geollyeoyo.
    By car it takes about an hour.
