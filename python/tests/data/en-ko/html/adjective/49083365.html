Korean

Etymology

Of native Korean origin. From 멋 (meot) + 이 (i) + 있다 (itda). Compare 맛있다 (maditda).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key)[mʌ̹ɕʰit̚t͈a̠]
  • Phonetic Hangul[머싣따]
Revised Romanization? meositda
Revised Romanization (translit.)? meosissda
McCune–Reischauer? mŏsitta
Yale Romanization? mesissqta

Adjective

멋있다 • (meoditda) (infinitive 멋있어, sequential 멋있으니)

  1. wonderful, nice, cool
